Proof of Existence is one of basic Blockchain services

It is an online service that verifies the existence of computer files, or any arbitrary statements as of a specific time via timestamped transactions in the blockchain, without revealing content of those resources

Content You provide must be unique, if what You provide was submited before transaction will fail

What it can be used for?

  • You can secretly put to the blockchain You predictions and reveal them when they will become true
  • You can run a lottery, by putting proof of secret numbers
  • You can put descriptions of Your inventions to prove You where first who invented it
